Disease Prediction

Leaf disease prediction is a crucial aspect of plant health management in agriculture and horticulture.It involves the use of various techniques,including machine learning, image processing, and data analysis, to predict the occurrence and severity of diseases affecting plant leaves.

Disease Information

Leaf disease information encompasses knowledge about various diseases that affect plant leaves,including their causes, symptoms, diagnostic methods, management strategies, and preventive measures. Leaf disease information is crucial for farmers, researchers, and gardeners to effectively identify, diagnose, and manage diseases.
